About Me

I'm a product manager with an unconventional path: Wall Street trader, startup founder, US Navy officer, and now building AI-native products at Microsoft. I've always been drawn to domains I don't yet understand, which means I've spent most of my career starting from scratch in unfamiliar territory and figuring it out fast.

What ties it together is curiosity, a compulsion to build, and the sense of satisfaction that comes from the pursuit of distinctly difficult challenges. I believe the best way to understand a problem is to make something, and that instinct has taken me across industries most PMs never touch. At Microsoft I've shipped products used by millions of advertisers...but I'm just as likely to be prototyping a side project on a weekend or pulling apart an engine on a classic truck to see how it works and posting a how-to video on YouTube to help others.
